What is Line OS?

Line OS is a Android Distrobution focused on speed, stability and simplicity. Based on the open-source Android ecosystem, it removes bloat, improves performance and gives you full control over your device.

Why Line OS?

Fast

Optimized system, less background processes, more speed.

Secure

Trust helps you understand the state of your device's security and warns you about possible threats.

Clean

No ads, no trackers, no unnecessary apps.

Latest Version

It upgrades your device's operating system. This allows your device to perform better.
